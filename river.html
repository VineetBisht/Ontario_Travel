Ottawa River Rafting


Great Weather & Perfect Water Levels This Year Summer fun with RiverRun Rafting

Last day of the 2018 season is September 16th Summer 2018 was ideal for rafting with it's great weather and perfect water levels. We have been busy renovating and landcaping. We now have free wifi at the Pavilion for all our rafting and kayaking guests Each year, more people return to River Run Rafting Resort to experience the incredible thrill of whitewater rafting on the Ottawa River. With River Run Rafting, you can choose the trip experience and thrill level that’s right for you!

New Rafting Adventure Video with GoPro Camera

Big Rafts & High Adventure

Our most popular raft trip is our 12 passenger High-Adventure Whitewater Raft Trip. This big raft offers lots of whitewater fun and is perfect for first-timers, groups and families…a little more raft and a great Canadian experience!

Sport Rafts & Ultimate Adventure

River Run pioneered the sport raft program on the Ottawa River over 20 years ago and we can confidently say “we do it best”. Our rafts are custom made for us by Sotar. Lighter in weight and with extra cross tubes, allowing you and our guides the maximum fun and experience available in whitewater!

Family Rafting

For the young and “young at heart”, join us for this gentlewhite water rafting trip on the Middle Channel of the river. With just enough whitewater to make it fun there’s plenty to see and learn about this historical river.

Our Resort & Accommodations

With over 4000 feet of beautiful shoreline on the Ottawa River, River Run Rafting offers the best location on the river. The resort property is the arrival point for your day of adventure with a short bus ride to put-in. Located just below the last set of major rapids you will arrive back at the resort for hot showers, your dry clothes, a cool refreshement at our lively pub while enjoying a video re-run presentation of your trip.
Camping and a choice of affordable accommodations are available to overnight stays.
